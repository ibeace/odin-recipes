My best Tomatoes recipe for now

Image of my Tomatoes Salad recipe

This my tomatoes salad at it's finest form and very easy to prepare with the following ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • basil and olive oil
  • black pepper

This recipe can be made perfectly using the following directions below:

  1. Combine Tomatoes with basil and olive oil
  2. add red vinegar and salt
  3. add black pepper and toss until evenly coated
